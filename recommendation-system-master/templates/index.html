Departments

All departments and career domains provided within the university are given below

Computer Sciences

The Department prepares tech-savvy graduates taking a scientific and practical approach to computation and its applications.

Business Studies

Department of Business Administration programmes are designed to cater to the needs of globalized corporate environment.

Engineering Studies

This program, rich in engineering science, prepares students for employment as electrical or electronics engineers.

Fashion Design

Fashion and Design aims to provide knowledge through an intensive curriculum to encompass the latest developments in the industry.

Media Studies
